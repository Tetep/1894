Why I Built This

Because ACA Liberty Needs Support That Actually Helps

When we talked last week, you told me the one thing ACA Liberty needs most is to rent this facility out. I took that seriously.

You've been carrying this place with so much heart and so little help, and I wanted to build something that finally supports the mission you've been fighting for.

I Want to Build Something Bigger Here in Liberty

I'm planning to base myself here, work out of Liberty, and build a network and community around this location. If I'm financially invested with ACA Liberty, I can commit fully - not just to the facility, but to the people who will walk through these doors.

A Place Where Entrepreneurs Can Grow Together

My plan is to host workshops, bring in other entrepreneurs, and create a space where people can learn, collaborate, and grow their businesses together.

  • Entrepreneur meetups
  • Workshops
  • Business growth sessions
  • Networking events
  • Community collaboration

The structure

The deal, in plain language.

01

Zero upfront.

I take the risk on the build. You owe nothing the day we start.

02

35% of all bookings.

Every booking that runs through the system. I only earn when you do.

03

One-year contract with a 3-month review.

90 days in, we sit down and look at what actually happened.

04

Every booking runs through Ninja-360.

One system. Both of us see the same numbers in real time.

05

Early cancel after the review means remaining contract value owed.

Once we're past the 90-day mark, we're committed. Fair to both sides.

The 90-day exit

Your escape hatch.

If it's not working at 90 days, you walk. Free and clear. No remaining balance. No hard feelings.

That's your exit. It exists because the deal has to be fair to you before it's fair to me. If I haven't proven the system works in 90 days, you owe me nothing.

After the review, we're committed for the year because by then we'll both know whether it's working.

Full transparency

You see what I see.

Every booking. Every dollar. Every report. No black box. No "trust me." We agree on the checks and balances up front, in writing, before we start.

  • All numbers visible to both of us in real time
  • Every booking documented and traceable
  • Built-in checks and balances we agree on together
  • If a number ever feels off, we sit down and look at the data
  • Everything in writing before day one
. . .

This isn't a vendor pitch. It's a partnership. The building already does the hard part: the history, the space, the location. My job is to make sure the right people find it, feel it, and book it. If I don't deliver in 90 days, you walk.
